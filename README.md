# Privacy Policy for Tracker 365

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

## Overview

Tracker 365 is a personal life management app that helps you track finances, goals, tasks, charity, personal growth, and daily journaling — all privately on your device.

---

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device only.** Nothing is sent to any server, cloud, or third party.

The app stores the following data locally in a SQLite database on your device:

- **Financial data** — income, expenses, categories, accounts (multi-currency), debts
- **Goals** — goal titles, descriptions, progress, milestones/steps
- **Tasks** — to-do items, priorities, due dates, recurring task templates
- **Charity** — monthly budgets, donation records, recipients, notes
- **Journal entries** — text entries, mood, date
- **Tips** — built-in and custom personal development tips, favorites
- **Settings** — your name, default account, app preferences
- **Notifications state** — last notification date (to avoid duplicate alerts)

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any personal data
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not use analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools
- We do not require account registration or login
- We do not share any data with third parties

---

## Data Export

The app allows you to export your financial transactions as a CSV file using your device's native share dialog. This export is entirely user-initiated and controlled — the file is not uploaded anywhere automatically.

---

## Notifications

The app schedules local push notifications to remind you about tasks, goals, and daily tips. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external notification servers beyond the standard OS notification service.

---

## Data Security

Since all data stays on your device:
- Your data is as secure as your device itself
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data
- No backup or recovery is possible after uninstallation

---

## Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. It does not collect any personal information from any user, including children.
